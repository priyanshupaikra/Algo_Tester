Moving Average Crossover

A classic momentum strategy that generates buy signals when the short-term moving average crosses above the long-term moving average.

Best for: Trending markets with clear directional moves.

Parameters: Short window (fast MA) and long window (slow MA).

Mean Reversion

A contrarian strategy using Bollinger Bands that buys when price hits the lower band and sells when it hits the upper band.

Best for: Range-bound markets with high volatility.

Parameters: Window (period) and standard deviation multiplier.
